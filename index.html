About Calvenn

Built in the Boardroom.
Forged in the Field.

Calvenn Starre is a senior operating executive who has spent over two decades launching, scaling, and stabilizing organizations across health & wellness, consumer products, insurance, and energy — driving more than $24M in first-year revenue at companies where vision met discipline.

As a COO, he has built operational infrastructure from scratch, trained leadership networks, and guided companies through rapid growth — all while maintaining governance and culture. Today, he takes those lessons directly to leaders, teams, and organizations through keynote speaking, executive training, and 1-on-1 coaching.

  • EOS Entrepreneurial Operating System
