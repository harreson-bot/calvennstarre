The Story

Who Is Calvenn Starre?

Calvenn Starre is a senior operating executive with 25+ years of experience launching, scaling, and stabilizing organizations across health & wellness, consumer products, insurance, and energy.

He didn't come to leadership through a classroom — he earned it in the field. As Chief Operating Officer for multiple companies, he built operational infrastructure from scratch, designed demand-generation systems, led leadership networks across the country, and scaled startups from concept to millions in first-year revenue.

At Amega Worldwide, he recruited and trained a leadership network that generated $4M per month within 90 days — delivering $24.6M in first-year sales. At FITTEAM Global, he launched company systems on schedule and on budget, hitting $8M in Year 1 and $16M in Year 2. At multiple other ventures, he built the operational backbone that turned early-stage vision into disciplined execution.

Today, Calvenn brings that same intensity and precision to speaking and coaching. Whether on a main stage, in a boardroom training, or in a 1-on-1 coaching session, his goal is simple: to give people and organizations the tools, frameworks, and mindset to become what they were built to be.

"You don't build great companies. You build great people — and great people build great companies."

— Calvenn Starre

His work spans multiple disciplines: leadership development, operational architecture, communication training, personal development, and purpose-driven leadership rooted in faith and spirituality. He doesn't just teach what sounds good — he teaches what he's lived.
